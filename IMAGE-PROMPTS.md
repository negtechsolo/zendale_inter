# IMAGE-PROMPTS.md — Zendale Photography Generation Guide

One prompt per required photo. Generate each image, save it with the **exact
filename** shown, drop all files into `/src/assets/images/`, rebuild — done.

## How to use these prompts

1. **Paste the Global Style Block below at the end of every prompt.** It keeps
   all 29 images looking like one photographer shot them for one brand.
2. **Set the aspect ratio listed per image** (e.g. `--ar 3:2` in Midjourney, or
   choose the matching ratio in your generator). Generate at the largest size
   available; the site downscales, never upscales.
3. **Faces:** African/Nigerian subjects; candid working expressions, never
   posed grins at the camera. No identifiable patients in clinical scenes —
   patients turned away, out of focus, or represented by equipment/hands.
4. If your generator supports negative prompts, add:
   `no text, no watermark, no logos, no smiling at camera, no posed stock photo look, no cartoonish colors, no plastic skin`
